Welcome to Surge, an application developed by averyio ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. Surge is designed to function without requiring you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personally identifiable information directly with averyio. All data generated by your use of the app remains entirely on your local device unless otherwise specified below through Apple's standard services.

3. In-App Purchases

Surge is free to download but offers In-App Purchases.

Payment Processing: All In-App Purchases are processed directly and securely by Apple via the App Store.

Data Access: We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials. We only receive confirmation from Apple regarding whether a purchase was successful so that we can unlock the relevant features within the app.

For more information on how Apple handles your payment data, please review the Apple Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics and Usage Data

We do not use any third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.) within Surge.

We only have access to anonymized, aggregated analytics provided by Apple App Store Connect. This data is entirely controlled by your iOS device settings and is only shared with us if you have opted-in to share app analytics with developers.

This data may include:

  • App crash reports
  • Number of downloads and deletions
  • General usage metrics (e.g., sessions per active device)

This information is strictly used to identify bugs, improve the app's performance, and understand general user engagement. It cannot be used to identify you personally. You can change your analytics sharing preferences at any time in your iOS device Settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements).

5. Third-Party Services and Advertising

No Ads: Surge does not display any advertisements.

No Tracking: We do not use any third-party tracking software, advertising networks, or SDKs that collect data on our behalf.

Surge does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
